Welcome to Who's My Rep

Learn about your local politicians. This site aims to make government more transparent by collecting various data sources in one location for your analysis.

Instructions

Click a state or search for a zipcode for information on federal and state level senators and representatives. Then click the links above to which group you want to further look at.

After clicking the link, you can go through all the politicians in the group and look at individual stats on each, what their recent social media activity has been, and what news stories are currently going on about them

Just go ahead and try it, click a state!
